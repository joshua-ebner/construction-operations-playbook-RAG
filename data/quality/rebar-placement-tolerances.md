# QC-RBR-012: Rebar Placement Tolerances

**Document ID:** QC-RBR-012  
**Applies to:** Riverside Tower — all cast-in-place concrete  
**Owner:** Quality Control  
**Effective date:** 2025-01-15

## Tolerance table (before pour)

| Dimension | Specified | Tolerance |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Bar spacing | Per drawing | ± 1 in |
| Cover (bottom) | Per drawing | +3/8 in / −1/8 in |
| Cover (sides/top) | Per drawing | ± 3/8 in |
| Lap splice length | Per ACI 318 | No negative tolerance |

## Inspection frequency

- **Pre-pour inspection** required for every pour hold point
- Re-inspection after any rework or bar replacement
- Document with photos in QC field app before sign-off

## Hold conditions

Hold the pour if:
- Any critical bar is out of cover tolerance on bottom face
- Lap splice length is short by any amount
- Unapproved bar substitution on site
- Tie wire spacing exceeds 12 in on vertical bars

## Release authority

Only a **QC inspector or superintendent** may release rebar for pour after inspection.

## Coordination with safety

Rebar caps required on all vertical dowels above 4 ft height per **SAFE-FP-001**.

## Nonconformance

If rebar fails inspection, open NCR per **QC-NCR-100** before rework begins.
