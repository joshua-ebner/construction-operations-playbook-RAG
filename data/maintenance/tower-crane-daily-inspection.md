# MAINT-CRN-101: Tower Crane — Daily Inspection

**Document ID:** MAINT-CRN-101  
**Equipment:** Tower Crane TC-RT-01, Riverside Tower  
**Owner:** Field Operations / Equipment  
**Effective date:** 2025-01-15

## Pre-shift inspection checklist

- Verify load moment indicator (LMI) is functional
- Check wire rope for broken strands, kinks, or corrosion
- Inspect hook, latch, and safety clip
- Test all limit switches (hoist upper/lower, trolley travel)
- Confirm communication system (radio) is operational
- Walk the jib — check for ice, debris, or bird nests

## Hydraulic and mechanical

- Check slew bearing grease points
- Verify counterweight pins are seated and locked
- Inspect foundation anchor bolts (visual only — do not adjust)

## Out-of-service criteria

Tag crane **out of service** and notify superintendent if:
- Any broken wire rope strands visible
- LMI displays fault or is unresponsive
- Unusual noise from slew drive or hoist drum
- Wind speed exceeds 25 mph sustained

## Weekly PM (in addition to daily)

- Lubricate hoist drum per manufacturer schedule
- Check anemometer calibration sticker (must be current)
- Review crane log for repeat fault codes

## Coordination note

Inspection that requires climbing the tower must follow **SAFE-FP-001** fall protection requirements.

## Escalation

Contact crane vendor service if unresolved within 1 hour. Reference asset tag **TC-RT-01**.
