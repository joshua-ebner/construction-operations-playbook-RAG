# MAINT-CRB-010: Equipment Crib — Checkout Process

**Document ID:** MAINT-CRB-010  
**Applies to:** Riverside Tower — Field Operations crib  
**Owner:** Field Operations / Equipment  
**Effective date:** 2025-01-15

## Issue process

1. Scan badge at crib kiosk
2. Enter asset tag and part/tool number
3. Superintendent approval required for items over $500
4. Return items to crib by end of shift unless extended checkout approved

## Common field equipment

| Item | Location | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Rebar tying gun RTG-14 | Crib A-03 | Calibrated monthly |
| Concrete vibrator CV-08 | Crib A-07 | Check cord before issue |
| Torque wrench TW-250 | Crib A-12 | Cert sticker must be current |

## Calibration requirements

- Torque wrenches: cert must be within 90 days
- Rebar tying guns: inspect battery and tie wire before issue
- Do not issue equipment with expired calibration — tag and remove from crib

## After-hours access

Contact on-call equipment lead for emergency issue. Do not bypass the kiosk log.

## Lost or damaged equipment

Report to superintendent within the shift. Open equipment damage report **EDR-001** before next checkout.
